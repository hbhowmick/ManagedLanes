Welcome to the Managed Lanes Viewer

This tool allows users to explore and visualize congestion and origin-destination characteristics of MassDOT-managed lanes. To get started, either use the dropdowns in the upper right corner or use your mouse to click a section of roadway on the map. Upon selection, a popup will appear below the map with origin-destination information for the selected subcorridor.

Congestion can be explored by the hour. Origin-destinations can be explored by direction of the roadway.

Zoom/pan to a location on the map using your mouse or the +/- buttons and arrow keys.
